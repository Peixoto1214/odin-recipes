Galinhada

Description

This is yet another favourite. My mom used to make my brothers and I this dish. It is a fairly simple one and easy to prepare.

Prep time

35 Minutes

Cook Time

25 Minutes

Servings

6

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken breasts , cut into chunks
  • 2 cubes of chicken bouillon , diluted in Â½cup of water
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 5 tablespoons oil
  • 2 scallions , finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic , pressed
  • 2 cups rice , washed and drained
  • 2 tomatoes , peeled, cored and diced
  • 1 green bell pepper , deseeded and diced
  • 4 tablespoons peas
  • 1 tablespoon parsley , finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro , finely chopped
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the chicken, chicken bouillon, parsley, cilantro and lemon juice. Marinate for 2 hours.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and brown the chicken pieces and the marinade for 3 minutes at high heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook for 5 minutes over high heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the rice, saffron, tomatoes, pepper, and peas. Mix well and cover with 3 cups of boiling water.
  5. Stir well and cook covered for 15 minutes over low/medium heat, stirring gently so that the rice does not stick to the bottom of the pot.
  6. Add a little boiling water during cooking if necessary.
